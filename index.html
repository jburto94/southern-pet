About Us

We are locally owned pet supply shop and salon. Our goals are to help our customers shop smart for their pets. Ask our knowledgable staff about our products, and they will try their best to help you with your needs.

We own carry a wide range of dog supplies including: food, toys, bowls, collars, treats, crates, and much more. You can find supplies for your cats, reptiles and small animals as well.

Along with supplies, we also offer boarding for your dogs or cats. For dog grooming services, we have a full grooming area where we have a full-time groomer.

More About Our Grooming Service

Grooming

We offer a full salon service for your dogs. Our full time grooming specialist works each Tuesday through Saturday. If you are interested in setting up a grooming appointment, please call us for full information. Spots tend to fill up quickly, so we advise to call a week in advance for appointments.

Anytime that the groomer is here, we do accept walk in nail trims. Be sure to call ahead, in case the groomer is out. She also provides maintenance programs, which offer baths reduced price. Pricing starts at $10!
